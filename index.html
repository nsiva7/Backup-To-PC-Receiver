How to Backup Your Mobile Data to PC

Follow these simple steps to backup your mobile data wirelessly

1

Setup PC Receiver

Install and Launch Receiver

  • Download the B2PC Receiver application on your PC
  • Install and run the Receiver application
  • Select or create a destination folder where you want to save your backups
  • The Receiver will display a QR code on your screen
üí° Tip: Make sure your PC and mobile device are connected to the same WiFi network
2

Connect Mobile App

Scan QR Code to Connect

  • Open the B2PC mobile app on your phone
  • Tap on the "Scan QR Code" button in the app
  • Point your phone camera at the QR code displayed on your PC screen
  • Wait for the connection to establish (you'll see "Connected" status)
‚úÖ Success: Once connected, both devices will show connection status
3

Select Folders

Choose What to Backup

  • In the mobile app, tap "Add Folder" button
  • Browse and select the folders you want to backup (Photos, Videos, Documents, etc.)
  • You can add multiple folders to your backup list
  • Review your selected folders before proceeding
üìÅ Note: You can backup any accessible folder on your device
4

Start Backup Process

Transfer Your Files

  • Select the folders you want to backup from your list
  • Tap the "Start Backup" button
  • Watch the real-time progress on both mobile app and PC receiver
  • Wait for the backup to complete (both devices will show "Backup Completed")
üéâ Done! Your files are now safely backed up on your PC in the selected folder

Privacy Policy

Siva Nimmala has adopted this privacy policy for B2PC (Backup To PC).

Data Collection

B2PC is not collecting any information from users. The app is completely ad-free with no advertisements displayed.

Permissions Used

  • Storage Permission: Only used to access and backup files from your mobile device to your PC
  • Camera Permission: Only used to scan QR codes for device connection
  • Internet Permission: Only used for:
    • Local network communication between mobile app and PC receiver
    • Gathering in-app purchase information
    • Displaying social media links (GitHub, WhatsApp, Telegram) in the about screen
  • Microphone Permission: Only used for audio visualizations when enabled by user (optional feature)

No Analytics

No analytics or tracking tools are implemented in B2PC. Your usage data is completely private.

Third-Party Services

We use Google In-App Purchases to unlock and verify purchases of premium items. These services collect usage data in compliance with their Privacy Policies.

Data Types

Non-Personal Data: Information that does not directly identify you.

Personal Data: Personally identifiable information that specifically identifies you as an individual. B2PC does not collect or store any personal data.

Children's Privacy

If you are under 15 years of age or a minor, please ask your legal guardian's permission to use or access premium items by purchasing. Siva Nimmala takes children's privacy seriously and encourages parents and/or guardians to play an active role in their children's online experience at all times.

COPPA Compliance

We are in compliance with the requirements of COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not collect any personal information from anyone under 13 years of age or older.
